Breakpoints and bootstrap classes

Anchor menu

  • Extra small (< 576px): 12 columns wide
  • Small (⋝ 576px): 4 columns wide
  • Medium (⋝ 768px): 3 columns wide
  • Large (⋝ 992px): 2 columns wide
  • Extra large (⋝ 1200px): 3 columns wide

Bootstrap classes: class="anchor-col col-12 col-sm-4 col-md-3 col-lg-2 col-xl-3"

Body content

  • Extra small (< 576px): 12 columns wide
  • Small (⋝ 576px): 8 columns wide
  • Medium (⋝ 768px): 9 columns wide
  • Large (⋝ 992px): 9 columns wide
  • Extra large (⋝1200px): 7 columns wide

Bootstrap classes: class="col-12 col-sm-8 col-md-9 col-lg-9 col-xl-7"
