Pathway components

Pathways are the primary method of in-page navigation. They promote a link to another page or website as a tile within the context of the page. They include include a summary of content and purpose.

Link to high-value internal content or services on the page.

Inline pathway

Feature image pathway

Promote links to high-value content with a feature image.

Image pathway list

Provide a standard pathway to content with a supporting image.
