Guidelines for use

Purpose:

Used to display a stylised quote or important content.

When to use this component:

  • On pages that include content that is attributed to a specific person. Most often used as part of Customer Story pages.
  • On business information content pages to highlight an example or key point.

Content rules

  • Include full name, position and name of company if relevant for direct quotes.
