Guidelines for use.

Purpose:

The progress bar visually shows the user how far they have progressed through a process. Progress bars are not used for navigation.

When to use this component:

Un-named steps progress bar

This is the default progress bar and shows progress through an entire process.

Named steps progress bar

This progress bar shows the progress of sub-steps within a larger process. Naming each step makes it clear the progress refers to a particular step, rather than the whole process.
