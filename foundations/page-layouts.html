Full width layout

  • Page content spans the full 12 columns of the grid.
  • Elements within the content can span 1, 2 or 3 columns.
  • Typically used on index pages such as category and topic pages.

Desktop, tablet & mobile

Two thirds layout (with side menu)

  • Page content spans 8 columns of the grid on the right-hand side. The left-hand side menu spans 4 columns.
  • Most elements in the content area are 1 column wide although a few components can be used in 2 columns.
  • Used for information pages such as tutorial and 101 pages.

Desktop & tablet

Mobile

Two thirds layout (without side menu)

  • Content spans 8 columns of the grid and is centred in the page.
  • Most elements in the content area are 1 column wide although a few components can be used in 2 columns.
  • Used for information pages such as news articles.
